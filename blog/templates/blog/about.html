About CCRC

Cryptocurrency is not merely a type of currency but is becoming a platform based on its ability to function as a digital platform. Cryptocurrency allows to realize profit-sharing economy in a distributed network environment, thus, is acknowledged as a catalyst to change the industrial framework.

Accordingly, the need for human resources to develop and utilize cryptocurrency technologies is rapidly growing and the level of technology required by industries is increasing day by day. Nevertheless, no country is currently ready to supply the manpower and technology to satisfy these demands.

The objective of Cryptocurrency Research Center (“Center”) at Korea University is to educate human resources, develop technology, and establish systems in the field of cryptocurrency and blockchain. The Center is planning to strengthen competence by collaborating with domestic and foreign research and educational institutions while fully utilizing the educational, technological and political capabilities and resources of the Graduate School of Information Security of Korea University.
