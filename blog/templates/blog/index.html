Research Fields

Cryptocurrency issues generally fall under the technology sector, economic sector, and policy sector. A healthy eco-system for cryptocurrency can only be built by systematically and harmoniously resolving issues under these three sectors. The Center has three research teams to support this tri-sector structure.



Technology Research

Technology Research is responsible for proposing and verifying innovative technologies concerning matters such as consensus mechanisms, processing speed, and distributed processing.

Token Economy Research

Token Economy Research deals with practical economic matters including, among others, coin economy, financial items, incentive mechanism, and coin assessment.

Policy Research

Policy Research is responsible for resolving legal, economical, and social conflicts that can arise upon incorporating cryptocurrencies into institutions, and for studying reasonable measures allowing consumers, investors, developers, and the government to share benefits.
